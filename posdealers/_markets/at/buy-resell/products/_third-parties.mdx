### [FinanzOnline](/docs/posdealers/buy-resell/products/3rd-party/finanzonline-management)

*FinanzOnline* provides electronic access to the tax authorities for entrepreneurs and their representatives, including registration of signature creation units, notifications and checks for cash registers like receipts for a start, annual, or stop, [more details](/docs/posdealers/buy-resell/products/3rd-party/finanzonline-management).
